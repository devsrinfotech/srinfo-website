Email Marketing

Return of investment for Email Marketing can go up to 4440% Leverage the power of Email Marketing.

Email marketing is an excellent marketing tool, it can be used to sell products, communicate with your customers and to spread brand awareness. If done correctly, it has the potential to get you a lot of leads. There are many companies that only rely on email marketing for their revenue! That’s the power of email marketing but the baseline here is that it should be done correctly.
Copywriting

The content of the email should be valuable, entertaining and interesting. There are dozens of emails in your potential customer’s inbox, if you want your emails to be seen then the content has to be good.

Monitoring and analysis

Email marketing involves a lot of analysis. Every email campaign has to be analysed in terms of conversion rates, open rate , click rate, bounce rate and unsubscribe rate. Constant improvisations will be made from these insights.

Design

A well designed email can catch the eyes of the audience faster. Among all those regular emails, our aesthetically designed email will stand out. Design is what wraps the content and having a good design makes a lot of difference.

Spam Filtering

We make sure that the emails do not end up in spam lists. There are many factors behind an email ending up in a spam list, which includes content quality, email lists, email software, rules, and text image ratio.
