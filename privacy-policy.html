Our Privacy Policy

Through our privacy policy, we clearly clarify everything about how we collect information from website visitors. The details regarding how we use this information is also clearly mentioned here.

We care about your privacy as much as you do. We are fully committed to protecting and safeguarding the information of our website visitors. So, we have developed a privacy policy to communicate to you about how your personal information is collected, processed and used by SR Infotech.

How is the information used?

  • The information we have gathered is used to improve the overall functioning and user experience of the website.
  • The data we collect is used to notify you regarding any changes in our services or any other updates. This helps you to stay informed about everything from customer service, website structure and changes in the services provided.
  • Information is also used to promote our products and services or to update any other feature we have added.
  • All the information collected by us is used in legitimate ways as per the laws and regulations.
  • The information we collect is fully protected with security and safeguard measures. It is completely protected against theft, loss, unauthorised access or copying use.
  • We collect information regarding how you’ve used our website. This includes pages you visited, location, traffic and resources you accessed.
  • The information is retained to provide you with specific information regarding the service you requested or any other enquiries.

How is the information used?

  • All information is collected through legitimate and lawful means.
  • We collect information when you enquire about our services in different channels. We use that information to communicate with you and to provide customised proposals as per your requirements.
  • We collect the information you provide voluntarily through our website or any other channels.
  • We use cookies to help you navigate through the website flawlessly and maintain continuity.

Our Privacy Policy Parameter

Safety of personal information

We assure you that all the information we collect is protected and safe. It is not sold to any third parties for any reason whatsoever. Even if we are doing that, it will be with your consent and permission.

Information disclosure

  • We are fully responsible for protecting your data. We won’t disclose information to anyone except in a few situations mentioned below.
  • If the law authorities are demanding us to provide information legally, we might have to resort to providing them with information.
  • If we decide to sell our business or a part of it, we might be required to share information for the proper functioning of the company.

Any changes in the privacy policy will be informed to you via any communication channels and it will be updated on this page as well.
