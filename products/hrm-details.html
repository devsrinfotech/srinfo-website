Human Resource Management Software (HRM)

SR-HRMS is a comprehensive Human Resource Management Software designed to revolutionize the way you handle HR operations. With its powerful features and user-friendly interface, SR-HRMS empowers businesses of all sizes to streamline their HR processes, improve employee management, and foster a productive and engaged workforce. Whether you're a startup, a growing business, or an established enterprise, SR-HRMS is your go-to solution for HR excellence.

Key Features of SR-HRMS:

Employee Information Management: Maintain a comprehensive database of employee profiles, skills, qualifications, and performance history. Access all the information you need in a few clicks.

Features

Leave and Attendance Tracking: Simplify leave management and attendance tracking with SR-HRMS's automated system. Employees can apply for leaves, and managers can approve them hassle-free. SR-HRM automates notifications for leave applications and allows managers to review the status of the leave application.

Performance Appraisal and Feedback: Conduct smooth performance appraisals and provide feedback to employees using SR-HRMS. Foster continuous growth and development.

Training and Development: Identify skill gaps and organize training programs to upskill your workforce. SR-HRMS helps you nurture talent and invest in your employees' professional growth.

Payroll Management: Manage payroll efficiently with SR-HRMS. Ensure accurate and timely salary processing while adhering to taxation and compliance requirements.

Customizable Workflows: Tailor SR-HRMS to suit your specific needs. Customize workflows, forms, and processes to align with your organization's unique requirements.

Employee Leaderboard:

SR-HRMS introduces an exciting gamification element to encourage employee performance and engagement. Employees can earn Review Points and Energy Points based on their achievements and contributions to the organization. These points are used to rank employees on a dynamic leaderboard, which showcases the top performers for each month.

How it works

Review Points:

Employees earn Review Points through various performance-related activities, such as completing projects, receiving positive feedback from clients or colleagues, meeting targets, and demonstrating exceptional skills.

Energy Points:

Energy Points are accumulated when employees actively participate in training programs, team-building activities, and other learning opportunities that contribute to personal and professional growth. SR-HRMS allows managers to allocate these energy points.

Leaderboard Ranking:

The Leaderboard dynamically ranks employees based on their combined Review Points and Energy Points for the current month. Employees can view their rankings in real-time through the HRMS Pro dashboard.

Monthly Analysis for HR:

SR-HRMS provides HR professionals with comprehensive monthly analyses of the Leaderboard. Access valuable insights into employee performance trends, identify high-performing individuals, and recognize opportunities for improvement.

Premium Features:

Alerts and Reminders: Never miss a deadline or important HR task again. Receive timely alerts and notifications through various platforms like Slack and Discord.

Employee Self-Service Portal: Empower employees to access and update their information, apply for leaves, and view payslips through the self-service portal, promoting transparency and efficiency.

Role-Based Access Control: Ensure data security and confidentiality with role-based access control. Grant specific permissions to employees based on their roles and responsibilities.
